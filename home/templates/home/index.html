CURRICULUM


SoCSE believes in educating students through learning by doing. The curriculum involves well designed laboratory courses involving structured and open ended experiments. Theoretical learning in the courses is enhanced by course project and integrated projects.


INFRASTRUCTURE


The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) was set up in 1983 with B.E. programme. Since then, the department has grown exponentially over the years on all fronts. The department has seven computer centres with around 300 computers equipped with the latest hardware, software and peripherals. A new feather in the cap is the recently established state-of-the-art AI & ML lab and High performance computing lab which delivers unparalleled performance for analytics, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and modern High-Performance Computer (HiPC). The department hosts a national level conference in 2019 with top companies.


PLACEMENTS


Students receive offers through placements/internships from reputed companies such as Samsung R&D, Infosys, Mercedes Benz R&D India, Microsoft R&D, , Deloitte and several others. Almost 90% of the students got placed. The highest salary package offered in our course was 30 LPA in Amazon for the role of software development. The average salary package offered was around 4 LPA-5 LPA.
